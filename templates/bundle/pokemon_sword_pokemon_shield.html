Pokemon Sword and Shield

Pokemon Sword and Shield comes out on November 15th, 2019 on Nintendo Switch. There will be over 800+ pokemon including the original 151 pokemon back in 1996. This guide will share the main differences and similarities between these two games.

(New) Dynamax

New evolution, Dyanamax, increases a Pokemon size and stats in battle as well as changing their moves. Trainers are only able to Dynamax their Pokemon if they have the Dynamax Band. This will only lasts for three turns and can only be performed in certain areas, such as Gyms.

Gigantamax

Gigantamax increases the Pokemon's appearance, size, stats and allows them to use G-Max Move in battle, but only certain Pokemon can perform a Gigantamax evolution.

(New) Max Raid Battles

New cooperative feature that allows up to four players to challenge a Dynamax Pokemon together.
